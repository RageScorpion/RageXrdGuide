    Hard
    956214K > f.S > 2H > 546P~K > 236S~S~H > 66 > 2S > 2H > ender (546PP / 236236H~P)

    Damage: 225

    This combo is hard not cause of the execution but because of the way you have to hit f.S. Most of the time, you will get c.S instead cause you're too close. Be too far away and Zekkei will whiff. If you happen to get c.S, you can go for other c.S starter routes instead.
