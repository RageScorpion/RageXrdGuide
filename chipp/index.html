    Difficulty is considered without the IAD c.Geki (956214K) input. Ordered from most damaging routes to least damaging.

    IAD Carded Geki

    c.S | Everywhere

    Extreme
    956214K > 66 > c.S > 2H > 546P~K > 236S~S~H > 66 > 2S > 2H > ender (546PP / 236236H~P)

    Damage: 266

    Fullscreen combo route is a frameperfect, dash in for 6f or 7f if hit crouching, 2H into Hochifu can't be delayed, same with Zekkei into 546P~K.

    The corner route is less hard, dash a tiny bit, no delay Zekkei.

    5K | Everywhere

    Medium
    956214K > 66 > 5K > 2H / (1) > 546P~K > 236S~S~H > 66 > 2S > 2H > ender (546PP / 236236H~P)

    Damage: 253 / 251

    Dash a tiny bit forward so Zekkei will hit Chipp, no delay Zekkei.

    2P | Everywhere

    Medium
    956214K > 66 > 2P > 2H / (1) > 546P~K > 236S~S~H > 66 > 2S > 2H > ender (546PP / 236236H~P)

    Damage: 245 / 246

    Fullscreen, dash a tiny bit forward, no delay Zekkei.
    For the corner route, you want to dash in deep, no delay Zekkei.

    The 1-Hit route only works in the corner, here, you want to dash a tiny bit forward as well, no delay Zekkei.

    2PP | Everywhere

    Medium-Hard
    956214K > 66 > 2PP > 2H / (1) > 546P~K > 236S~S~H > 66 > 2S > 2H > ender (546PP / 236236H~P)

    Damage: 224 / 229

    Fullscreen, dash in deep, just before it would OTG, no delay Zekkei.
    For the corner route, you want to dash in deep, no delay Zekkei.

    Fullscreen 1-Hit, dash in a bit, but not too deepm, no delay Zekkei.
    Corner 1-Hit, dash in deep, no delay Zekkei.

    Difficulty is considered without the Kara c.Geki (236S214KK) input. Ordered from most damaging routes to least damaging.

    Kara Carded Geki

    c.S | Everywhere

    Medium
    236S214KK > 66 > c.S > 2H / (1) > 546P~K > 236S~S~H > 66 > 2S > 2H > ender (546PP / 236236H~P)

    Damage: 246 / 254

    Fullscreen 2-Hit does not work. For the corner route, dash in deep, no delay Zekkei.

    Fullscreen and corner 1-Hit, dash in and hit Chipp at the highest moment after Geki hit ,no delay Zekkei.
    Note: You can also go for 2S starter instead, but it will deal less damage.

    Hard
    956214K > 66 > c.S > 2H / (1) > 546P~K > 236S~S~H > 66 > 2S > 2H > ender (546PP / 236236H~P)

    Fullscreen 2-Hit is a frame perfect, dash 7f, delay Zekkei by 2f.

    Fullscreen 2-Hit is not worth it, all the other routes are duable. Cause of that I won't set the difficulty to extreme.
