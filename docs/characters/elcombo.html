IAD Geki

5K | Corner only

Extreme
956214K > 546(whiff) > 5K > 2H(1) > 546 dl P~K > dl 236S~S~H > 66 > 2S > 2H > ender (546PP / 236236H~P)

Damage: 211

Frame perfect combo on Elphelt with IAD Geki combo starter. Only works in the corner. You have to delay IAD Geki enough, otherwise Elphelt will be too low and Zekkei will connect on the first frame. Elphelt will be too high and the Puff Puff will whiff.

After the 546(whiff) into the 5K, input 5K as 4K to be on time. You must delay the 546~P by 1, 2 or 3 frames. If you don't delay it or delay by 4f, it will connect on first frame.
For the PuffPuff, there must a delay for the ~S from 236S by at least 2 frames. You can delay it by a maximum of 4. This makes it a 3f timing
Thanks to Kkots for finding this route
